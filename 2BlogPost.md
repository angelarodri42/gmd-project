# Executive Summary

**Title:** Midnight Munchies
**Concept Statement:** A multi-level game of an endless runner that needs to munch (eat chocolate) in order to attack the enemies. If no chocolate no fuel, but maybe she can jump over them….
**Genre:** Arcade-pixel art endless runner
**Target Audience:** casual players, all audiences
**Unique Selling Points:**
Different ways to deal with enemies: jumping or attacking them
Simple controls: just jump, and attack

## Gameplay

**Objectives:** survive for as long as possible
**Game progressions:** the speed increases over time and more enemies spawn
**In-Game GUI:** a scoreboard in a corner, and some chocolate indicator
**Core Loop:** the character runs to the right automatically while enemies appear to the new “land” that the girl runs to. In the “new” land there is also chocolate spawn there, the player can collect it and it will be added to the chocolate indicator. The player can jump over the enemies or just “hit them with a chocolate ball” to defeat them. To get the attack mode the player needs will use the chocolate in the indicator and it will eventually run out if no chocolate is collected. The score increases the longer you survive the higher it gets. 

## Mechanics

**Jumping:** jump to avoid enemies and be defeated!
Chocolate Attack: collecting chocolate fills up the chocolate indicator bar, which allows the player to activate the attack mode. In the attack mode, the player can attack with a ball of chocolate
**Collision:** happens when you hit an enemy, and you are defeated!
